About AgriLink Malawi

AgriLink Malawi is a digital platform connecting farmers directly with buyers across Malawi. Our goal is to eliminate middlemen, ensure fair pricing, and promote sustainable agriculture through technology. We believe in empowering local farmers by giving them the tools to access a broader market, and providing buyers with a reliable source of quality, traceable produce.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I register as a farmer or buyer?

Click the "Login" button on the homepage, then select "Don't have an account? Register". You can choose your role during the simple registration process.

Is there a fee to use AgriLink?

Basic registration and listing are free. We may take a small commission on completed transactions to maintain and improve the platform.

How are payments handled?

Payments are processed securely through our integrated payment gateway partner, PayChangu, which supports local mobile money services like Airtel Money and TNM Mpamba.
